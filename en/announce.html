Call for Proposal : K-Startup Grand Challenge 2023

KIC SV has advanced entrepreneurial and business success through numerous incubation/acceleration programs in its history, the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) of 2023 K-startup Grand Challenge event rest upon successful promotion of program and preparation of America audition. Based on these needs, KIC SV is inviting proposals from qualified respondents with a large entrepreneurship network to recruit the participating startups from America. We would like to encourage interested companies to present proposals and look forward to working with you.

Please read the information in this packet carefully and provide your proposal accordingly by 5:00PM (PDT) on Wednesday, March 22, 2023. To be eligible for consideration, the completed proposals should be submitted via email(sonkim@kicsv.org) by the deadline.
